Description
I released this at the Intro competition at The Party-92, but noone understood it was actually a bootintro (just 1024 bytes of code, including boot-header and exit-code). The logo was designed by Mårten by cutting rectangles out of elipses and the music was recited to me over the phone by Erik. Also note that the vector transforms from a point to a line to a cube :)
I spent a lot of time making the code as short as possible. The sinetable for the vector is for the first 4th, the rest is flipped. The coords for the cube is 1 bit per vertex. The instrument is actually a piece of the copperlist...
